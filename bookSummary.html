Book Summary

  • So basically...
  • The Things They Carried is a collection of stories from the Vietnam war. The stories told in this book are described as fictional, however they seem to relate to some of the real experiences Tim O'Brien, the author, was witness to during his time spent in Vietnam during the Vietnam War. The 22 chapters all represent different stories about the war and life as a soldier, however they are all related with many of the characters being featured throughout a lot of the short stories.
