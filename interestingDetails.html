Interesting Details

  • The author, Tim O'Brien, was drafted into the Army during the Vietnam War, and thus served two years of service in Vietnam. He witnessed many real, life-changing experiences that are very similar to the fictional novel he writes.
  • Tim O'Brien also published a memoir about his life entitled If I Die in a Combat Zone, Box Me Up and Take Me Home. The memoir discusses his Vietnam experience, and many characters seem to be very similar to those in The Things They Carried.
  • Some of the novel was first published in the magazine Esquire in the late 1980's before it was officially published as a complete work.
